KLG Transportation Services

KLG Transportation offers a competitive assortment of same day delivery services to help all the communities of Southern California. Within a matter of hours, our bonded drivers can deliver all sorts of packages ranging from your typical retail box and envelope deliveries to more specialized types of parcels involving our medical courier services, legal courier services, and same day freight delivery. No job is too great for the likes of us—we can even transport up to 10,000 pounds of palletized cargo in the same day with the help of our dedicated freight vehicles and drivers. By serving you with our speedy fleet of sedans, cargo vans, box trucks with lift gates, and powerful freight trucks—we’re always equipped and prepared to deliver!
